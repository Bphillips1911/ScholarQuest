🎯 OVERVIEW

The PBIS House of Champions is a cutting-edge web-based platform that creates an engaging, competitive environment where students earn points for academic excellence, positive behavior, and attendance. Built on the proven Positive Behavioral Interventions and Supports (PBIS) framework, our system fosters school pride, improves student outcomes, and strengthens the home-school connection.
